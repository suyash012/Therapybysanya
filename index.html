Our Services

Individual Therapy

"Are you feeling blue? Do you need a place to vent out? Join me for an individual session!"

In the therapy sessions, we will work towards understanding your concerns and will work out a way to solve the same! It's going to be a mix of venting out and skill building!

Couples Counseling

"And they lived happily ever after... Cannot resonate with this? Fret not! After all, life is not a movie!"

If you feel your relationship requires work, join me for a couple-therapy session! We will discuss the concerns that you both are facing and learn how to communicate better.

Family Therapy

"My parents don't understand me" "I cannot stand my family"

If you resonate with these examples, looks like it is time to talk it out! Let's sit together and understand where is the gap!

Group Therapy

"Together, we are stronger."

Providing a supportive group environment to share experiences and learn from others.

Counseling for Parents

"Sometimes change in the environment can help our children."

Get in touch with me if you wish to speak about your child/children. Let's work together on understanding their concerns and helping them out!

Workshops & Seminars

"Knowledge is power, especially when it comes to mental health."

Educational sessions on various mental health topics for individuals, groups, and organizations.

All About Therapy

Therapy is a process intended to heal in a non-judgmental environment .

Absolutely not! I respect you unconditionally for the person you are!

Confidentiality

For adults: Everything you share here stays here. It's advisable to share a trusted person's contact for emergencies.

For children: I would need to be in touch with parents/guardians/trusted adults only to ensure your safety when I'm not around.

Therapeutic Orientation

I usually use an eclectic approach in my therapy sessions. This means that I use a combination of therapies.

Some of the therapies that I use are Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Behavioural Therapy, Art Therapy, Play Therapy, and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, among others.
